## Market research
1. Download a social media app
2. Follow accounts with shared passions
3. Search for hashtags or location tags
4. Save posts to reference later

## Identify your audience
1. What kind of person will follow me?
2. What are their interests and passions?
3. Why are they interested in following me?

## Ideal customer profile
1. Age
2. Gender
3. Geographic
4. Profession
5. Household income
6. Hobbies
7. Habits
8. Job titles

## Consistency
Content must be consistent. The goal is to post content that appeals to your audience
visually and aesthetically, and ties back to your brand's purpose.
